Our 5 Step Method

1) First we create online surveys for your business.

2) Second, you as the business owner give some sort of incentive to entice the customer to fill out the survey. A link for the surveys website would be given with the receipt.

3) Third, we analyze and conduct a statistical analysis to gain insights from the data

4) Forth, we create easy to read report that display the insights we found to see if changes created more satisfied and happy customers.

5) Then, we continue to keep track of your AB test, Usablity test and other UX test periodically to keep you informed with objective data and ways to increase customer satisfaction, reviews and customer acquisition.
