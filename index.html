"Bringing UX Research to Smaller Businesses!"

Why Us?

We want to help your business! UX research is usually only used for large corporations. We're on a mission to bring these research methods to the most important part to our economy, the small and medium business.

What Do We Do?

We come to your business, and use user research methods to help you gather data on your users and consumers. We then help you to find data on who your target audience is, and how to make their experience better with your business, product, service, or website

Whats The Purpose?

Our goal is to conduct research to help you understand who your customer or users are. We created this company becasue large companies found this out, but we want to help the small and medium companies to use these same methods so you can grow as well.

Our Method

  1. Qualitative Research:
      2. -Review business/product/service/website online
      -Review business/product in person
      -Collect subjective data via questionnaire and other methods
      -Speak with owner, fill out their issues in a questionnaire
  2. Create a Persona and User Story:
      3. -Persona: an example of the target audience/main user. Ex) age, gender, occupation
      -User Story: a user story tells the process the user goes through when they go into a business, uses a product, or service.
  3. Create a research Study:
      4. When then create questions and hypotheses(theories) of ways to fix the issues.
  4. Quantifiable Data: Collect objective data that we can use statistics to proove
      5. Ex) interviews, Surveys, AB testing, Usability Analysis
  5. Repeat processes:
      6. Repeat the data to ensure it is consistent, valid and accurate
  6. Analyze the data
      7. Use statistics to analyze the data to find trends, information and ways to help the business do better.
  7. Create a Report:
      8. We then create a report so business owners can determine the best methods to fix issues.
