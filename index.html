"Bringing UX Research to Small and Medium Businesses"

Our Mission

Now days, a lot of businesses focus a lot of time attempting to raise their online reviews, customer satisfaction and acquire more customers, but don't know how to collect or analyze actual data of their customers. This is where we come in. We use proper UX Research methods to collect and analyze data from the customers, then report it to the business owners in ways they can easily understand. Then business owners can use the insights from the data to increase online reviews, customer acquisition and customer retainment. Thus happier customers, equals more traffic, which equals higher income!

Our 5 Step Method

1) First we create online surveys for your business.

2) Second, you as the business owner give some sort of incentive to entice the customer to fill out the survey. A link for the surveys website would be given with the receipt.

3) Third, we analyze and conduct a statistical analysis to gain insights from the data

4) Forth, we create easy to read report that display the insights we found to see if changes created more satisfied and happy customers.

5) Then, we continue to keep track of your AB test, Usablity test and other UX test periodically to keep you informed with objective data and ways to increase customer satisfaction, reviews and customer acquisition.

Our 6 Point Scale

Our 6 Point Scale Our monthly growth is based on our scale of 6, a 6 is the best customer experience, while a 0 is a terrible customer experience.
